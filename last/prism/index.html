CEBU TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY

Cebu Technological University (CTU) is a state university in the Philippines renowned for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and community service. Established in 1911, CTU has grown into a leading institution that offers a wide range of academic programs, particularly in science, technology, engineering, agriculture, and management. The university has multiple campuses strategically located across Cebu Province, with its main campus situated in Cebu City. This multi-campus setup allows CTU to serve various communities and provide accessible education to students from different backgrounds. CTU emphasizes innovation and technological advancement, aligning its programs with the demands of the global market. It is recognized as a Center of Excellence and Development in several fields by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). In addition to its undergraduate and graduate programs, CTU is also active in research, extension services, and partnerships with industries, which further enhance its role as a catalyst for regional and national development. With a strong focus on producing skilled and socially responsible professionals, CTU continues to uphold its mission of fostering inclusive and transformative education.

      BS TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT

      The Bachelor of Science (BS) in Technology Management is an undergraduate program designed to equip students with a blend of technical knowledge and managerial skills. This degree bridges the gap between technology and business, preparing graduates to effectively manage technology-driven organizations and projects. It focuses on understanding technological systems, business strategies, and leadership principles, making it ideal for those aiming to pursue careers in technology management, project management, or leadership roles in tech-based industries.

      Program Structure and Core Subjects

      A BS in Technology Management typically combines courses from both business and technology disciplines. Students study subjects like operations management, organizational behavior, information technology, data analytics, and innovation management. Additionally, the program often includes courses on emerging technologies, such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and cybersecurity, to ensure students stay up-to-date with the latest advancements. The curriculum also emphasizes practical skills through hands-on projects, internships, and case studies. Students learn to analyze and solve real-world problems, manage teams, and make informed decisions using data and technology. Soft skills like communication, teamwork, and leadership are also a vital part of the training.
